Hobbies!

My personal hobbies page, where I talk about old tech, modding, retro games and other things I'm interested in!

Old Technology

I have always liked fixing and messing with older technologies. The first thing I ever fixed was my dad's walkman, and I spent a long time playing with it afterwards.

  • Grail Technology: IBM ThinkPads(T530)
  • Why I Want one: The durability, modularity, and customizability.

Modding

Modding is one of my favorite hobbies, as it allows me to replay my favorite games with new suprises. Also, modding different hardware to go past their original limits and actually being successful always feels good. Whether it's modifying an old console, adding custom firmware, or just adding some new things to Fallout it's always fun.

  • Types of Modding I Enjoy: Game modding, hardware tweaks, custom ROMs.
  • Recent Projects: Added a capture card to an old console to output video, working on my own rom for Pokemon Yellow.

One Piece

I'm a huge fan of One Piece, the manga by Eiichiro Oda. The story is full of themes of freedom, revolution, and justice in a way that I think is really well done.

  • Favorite Characters: Luffy, Chopper, Sanji, Robin
  • Best Arcs: Marineford, Wano, Skypiea, Egghead
  • Best Parts: The foreshadowing, world building, and deep lore.

Retro Games

Retro games are pretty much all I play. I really like RPGs, platformers, and story driven games. My favorite retro game is Warioland.

  • Favorite Retro Games: Super Mario 64, The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, Metal Gear Solid
  • Why I Like Retro Games: Unique gameplay mechanics, pixel-art graphics, usually heavier focus on writing.
